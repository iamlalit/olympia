to have twitter timeline, please follow these steps:

1) open this URL:
https://dev.twitter.com/web/embedded-timelines/user
2)Then there is a link:
"Create a new embedded user timeline"
3)Click on this link, it will redirect the user to login page of twitter widget.
4) Login into twitter account: enter email and pwd.
5)Once you authenticated widget page appears
6) after reviewing all the setting click on the button to create widget 
7) once all done it will give you a small script/widget
